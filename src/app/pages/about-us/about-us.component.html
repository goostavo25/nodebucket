About Us

Nodebucket was born after our group of developers got together to create an app where users could create, manage and delete tasks off a to-do list. After weeks in the works, the app was developed and optimized for easy access and use, with user-friendly features like drag and drop.
Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to organize the world one list at a time and make everybody efficient by completing tasks with our fantastic tool.
